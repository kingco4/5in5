Does Heaven have a ghetto?

Personaly I am not sure how to answer this question, but I guess we can start out by defining what heaven is and what a ghetto is.


Heaven Ghetto
a place regarded in various religions as the abode of god and the angels, and of the good after death, often traditionally depicted as being above the sky. A part of a cirt especially a slum area, occupied by a minority group or groups.

With that knowledge is there really a ghetto in heaven?

Considering that heaven is not regarded as a city and ghettos are usually areas in a city, I would say no heaven does not have a ghetto.
